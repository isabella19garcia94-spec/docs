Getting started with JSONViewer AI takes less than a minute and requires no setup.

## Step 1: Open the tool
Open JSONViewer AI in your web browser.

## Step 2: Paste your JSON
Copy your JSON data and paste it into the editor.

## Step 3: View formatted output
The tool automatically formats and validates the JSON.

## Step 4: Explore your data
Expand and collapse objects to inspect the structure easily.

That’s it — no account, installation, or configuration required.
